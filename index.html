ABOUT THE GAME

Rock, Paper, SCissors is a game of chance where you play against the computer to get as many points as you can.
The object of the game is to guess what the opponent will choose and hope that you win. There are many versions of this game as it a classic.
The rules are simple.


Rock beats Scissors.
Paper beats Rock.
And Scissors beats paper.
